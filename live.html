Life in San Sebastian

Getting ready for your new life in San Sebastian?

If you are currently in the city or planning to move in 4-6 weeks, there are several things you need to prepare for, which we will be willing to help you with!

Housing

Whether you are planning to stay here for a short or long period, we can help you find an appropriate room or apartment. We are in continuous contact with real state agencies and landlords and will find you something that fills your preferences and the price you want to pay. A few weeks prior to your arrival in San Sebastian, contact us and we will guide you through the contract process and ensure that everything is ready for you.

Living expenses

Quality of life and the overall cost of living are both significant draws, as is the beautiful seashore and all that yummy pintxos and kalimotxo. You need to know that San Sebastian is more expensive than others to live, work and play in than others in Spain. The cost of living in San Sebastian is 30% higher than the national average. Here are some data to get you an idea:

  • Monthly rent (1 bedroom apt.): 850 €
  • Monthly rent (room in shared house): 400 €
  • One-way bus ticket : 1.70 €
  • Utilities (gas,electricity,etc. in apt.): 160 €
  • Meal (inexpensive restaurant): 12 €
  • Meal for two (three-course menu): starting from 50 €
